Movement options
1. Track within unity distance and translate into real world distance
	- this distance is used to determine walls to stop movement of Cozmo when either
	  the place where the walls are, or to stop movement inputs sent to Cozmo when
	  walls are hit
2. Take input when key is pressed to move cozmo forward, and stop Cozmo's movement when 
   key is unpressed.
